Death

Death Metal Virtuosos

The Lineup

Initially called Mantas, Death was founded by Chuck Schuldiner, Frederick DeLillo, and Barney Lee.

The Music

Death is considered one of the best heavy metal bands, as well as a pioneer of the death metal subgenre.

The Story

Death's first album, Scream Bloody Gore, was released in 1987. Their final album, The Sound of Perseverance, was released in 1998. The band was disbanded in 2001 after Chuck Schuldiner died.
